DESCRIPTION

In this project i have used same color tones to make it look subtle. Loud color schemes can sometimes put off people and do not feel great in the long run. So, the lighter and subtle colors work well in the long run. I have also used 3d Effects to create some real depth. I have used different shapes and techniques to make my digital art work look more fun and interesting. when i do this art piece i had to visualize on how i would make shapes to look more 3d in perspective view.
