TITLE

KOI THE

MEDIUM

Adobe illustrator

DESIGN ELEMENT

Line, Plane & Shape, Dots

DESCRIPTION

In this project i did Poster for koi The company. Its a bubble tea shop which has 20 branches all around Malaysia. KOI BB Bear is the main mascot for KOI THE. The overall look I want my artwork to look vintage and have straightforward information. I have used simple fonts and Use composition techniques that are in line with whats naturally pleasing to the eye of customers. The main highlight and goal are to promote KOI THE along With the collaboration in celebrating National Hero Day. The main is to appreciate and celebrate the contribution of our police force, doctors, nurses, and firefighters during the pandemic time. So I would like to honor and thank all the heroes in the world who helped and sacrifices their lives for saving people's lives and trying their level best to keep the country in control. In this poster the tone of voice is Relax, Friendly, Thankful, Optimistic, and Excitation. I have also used Warm color, Cool color, Monochromatic colors, Summer colors, and Milky brown colors to complete this poster.
